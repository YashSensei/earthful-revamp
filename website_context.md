# 🌿 Earthful — Full Context Overview

## 1. What Earthful is
Earthful (earthful.me) is an India-based clean-label nutrition brand focused on plant-based supplements and wellness products.

- Core idea: “Nutrition from nothing but nature”
- Category: Health & wellness / supplements
- Positioning: Natural alternative to synthetic supplements
- Mission: Make nutrition simple, honest, and non-intimidating

---

## 2. Founders & Background
- Founded in 2020
- Founders:
  - Veda Gogineni
  - Sai Sudha Gogineni
- Background: IIT Kharagpur alumni
- Origin: Started during COVID to create clean alternatives to synthetic supplements

---

## 3. Core Mission
Earthful aims to solve a major problem:

### Problem:
- Supplements are synthetic
- Hard to understand
- Full of additives

### Solution:
- Plant-based ingredients
- Simple formulations
- Transparent labeling
- Focus on preventive health

---

## 4. Product Categories

### Main Offerings:
- Multivitamins (kids, adults, women 40+)
- Plant-based protein powders
- Condition-specific supplements:
  - Menopause support
  - PCOS
  - Sleep & stress
  - Hair, skin, bone health

### Product Philosophy:
- 100% plant-based
- No synthetic vitamins
- Minimal ingredients (2–7 ingredients)
- Clean-label (no fillers or additives)

---

## 5. Unique Differentiation

- Early mover in India’s clean-label supplement space
- Strong focus on women’s health (especially menopause)
- Uses real food sources (algae, chickpea, herbs)
- Combines product + education + community

Example:
- India’s first herbal multivitamin for menopausal women

---

## 13. TL;DR

Earthful is a plant-based, clean-label nutrition startup from India, founded in 2020 by two IIT alumni sisters. It offers natural supplements (multivitamins, protein, women’s health products) made from real food sources instead of synthetic ingredients. The brand focuses on transparency, simplicity, and preventive health, with a strong emphasis on women’s wellness. It operates as a D2C brand and aims to build trust in clean nutrition.
